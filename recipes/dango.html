Sweet

An Dango

Prep 20 mins / Cook 10 mins / Serves 4

Dango should be simple: soft, springy rice dumplings with a clean sweetness. This version keeps the dough neutral and lets glossy anko do the work.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup shiratamako or mochiko rice flour
  • 1/4 cup joshinko rice flour, or more mochiko
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/2 cup warm water, plus more as needed
  • 1 cup sweet red bean paste
  • Pinch of fine salt

Method

  1. Mix the flours, sugar, and salt in a bowl. Add warm water slowly until the dough feels like soft clay.
  2. Knead for 2 minutes, then divide into 16 small balls.
  3. Boil the dumplings until they float, then cook for 2 minutes more.
  4. Transfer to cold water for 1 minute, then drain well.
  5. Thread onto skewers or serve in small bowls. Spoon red bean paste over the top.

Finish

Serve at room temperature. If the dumplings firm up, warm them briefly with steam before adding the anko.
