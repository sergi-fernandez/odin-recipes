Sushi Rice

Prep 10 mins / Cook 20 mins / Makes 4 cups

Sushi rice is less about complicated technique and more about restraint. Rinse well, season while warm, and fold gently so the grains stay glossy.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups short-grain Japanese rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon fine salt

Method

  1. Rinse the rice until the water runs mostly clear, then drain for 10 minutes.
  2. Cook rice with water in a rice cooker or covered saucepan until tender.
  3. Warm vinegar, sugar, and salt just until dissolved.
  4. Transfer hot rice to a wide bowl and drizzle seasoning over it.
  5. Fold with a spatula while fanning until the rice is glossy and just warm.

Finish

Keep the rice covered with a damp towel. Do not refrigerate it before rolling; cold rice turns firm and dry.
