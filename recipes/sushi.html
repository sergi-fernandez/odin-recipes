Sushi Rolls

Prep 35 mins / Cook 20 mins / Makes 4 rolls

Homemade sushi is easiest when you keep the fillings modest and the rice layer thin. These rolls are crisp, creamy, and forgiving.

Ingredients

  • 4 sheets nori
  • 3 cups prepared sushi rice
  • 1 cucumber, cut into thin strips
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 200 g crab stick, cooked shrimp, or tofu
  • Pickled ginger, wasabi, and soy sauce for serving
  • Bowl of water for your hands

Method

  1. Place a nori sheet shiny-side down on a bamboo mat or clean towel.
  2. With damp hands, spread a thin layer of rice over the nori, leaving 2 cm clear at the top.
  3. Arrange cucumber, avocado, and protein in a narrow line across the lower third.
  4. Roll firmly from the bottom, using the mat to keep the shape tight.
  5. Rest seam-side down for 2 minutes, then slice with a wet sharp knife.

Finish

Do not overfill the rolls. A lean line of filling gives cleaner slices and better texture.
