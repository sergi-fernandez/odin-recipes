Grilled

Yakitori

Prep 20 mins / Cook 15 mins / Serves 4

Yakitori is at its best when the chicken is cut evenly and glazed in layers. This version uses thigh meat for juiciness and scallions for sweetness.

Ingredients

  • 600 g boneless chicken thighs, cut into bite-size pieces
  • 6 green onions, cut into 3 cm lengths
  • 8 bamboo skewers, soaked for 20 minutes
  • 1/3 cup soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup mirin
  • 1/4 cup sake
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • Neutral oil for the grill or pan

Method

  1. Simmer soy sauce, mirin, sake, and sugar for 8 minutes, until lightly syrupy.
  2. Thread chicken and green onion alternately onto skewers.
  3. Heat a grill pan or broiler and oil it lightly.
  4. Cook skewers, turning often, until the chicken is nearly done.
  5. Brush with tare and cook for another 2 to 3 minutes, glazing each side.

Finish

Keep a little tare aside before brushing raw chicken. Use that clean portion for a final glossy coat at the table.
